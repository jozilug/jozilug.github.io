The When and the Where

We meet on the 3rd Monday of every month in Joburg.

New members are always welcome! Bring a friend! Bring two!

The What

Each meetup starts at 18:30 and consists of a short, accessible talk on a topic related to Linux or Free or Open Source software (FOSS), followed by dinner and drinks.

Stick around afterward and chat with fellow geeks enthusiasts.

The Why

We want to grow the GNU/Linux and FOSS community in South Africa through informal chats about the technical topics that interest us.

We're always looking for new contributors. Let us know if you'd like to give a talk, suggest a topic, or organize a event.
